Fruit Salad

Description

Fruit Salad is delicious and easy to make!

First you will need to gather the ingredients then follow the steps to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Blackberries
  • Blueberries
  • Any other fruit!

Steps

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Wash all Fruit
  3. Peel if neeeded
  4. Cut Fruit into bite-size pieces
  5. Mix in a big bowl
  6. Enjoy!
