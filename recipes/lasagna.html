Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is delicious and easy to make!

First you will need to gather the ingredients then follow the steps to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Mix Sauce and Noodles together in a baking dish
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top
  4. Bake till golden brown
  5. Enjoy!
