Soup

Description

Soup is delicious and easy to make!

First you will need to gather the ingredients then follow the steps to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Veggies
  • Stock
  • Shredded Chicken

Steps

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Cut up Veggies
  3. Put Veggies, Stock, and Shredded Chicken in a Pot
  4. Bring to a Boil
  5. Place a lid on the pot and turn the temperature to low
  6. Cook for hours so the flavors meld
  7. Enjoy!
